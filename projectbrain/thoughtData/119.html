"Bubbles"

 

An interactive installation at the ZKM, Karlsruhe, Germany.

 

Produced by Wolfgang Muench and Kiyoshi Furukawa in 2000.

 

Using your shadow you can interact with the bubbles being projected on a big screen. You can gently push them or lift them up or pop them, depending on the speed of your movement.
